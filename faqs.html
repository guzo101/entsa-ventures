Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about ENTSA savings plans and worker placement services.

How does the daily savings program work?

You choose a daily slot (K100, K200, K300) or request a custom plan. You then save consistently based on your selected amount.

Can I request a custom savings amount?

Yes. ENTSA can structure a custom daily amount based on your monthly or target savings goal.

Who can join ENTSA?

Anyone with income can join, including traders, employees, entrepreneurs, freelancers, and part-time workers.

Does ENTSA offer worker placement?

Yes. ENTSA places trained domestic and general workers for households and organizations, both part-time and full-time.

How do I get started?

Call 0979415130, email info@entsaventures.com, or use the contact form to request onboarding support.
